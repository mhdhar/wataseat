Captain? Here's your guide

Start earning in 5 minutes — no tech skills needed

1

Message the bot

Send any message to WataSeat on WhatsApp. Enter your name, boat name & license.

2

Connect payments

Type /connect to set up Stripe. Complete ID & bank verification — payments go directly to you.

3

Create a trip

Type /trip — pick type, date, time, seats, minimum & price. Upload a vessel photo.

4

Share with your group

Forward the booking link to your WhatsApp group. Guests tap to book & pay securely.

5

Get paid automatically

When the threshold is met, payments are captured. 90% to you, 10% platform fee.
